The Cosmopolitan

Although putatively created in 1975 in Minneapolis, the Cosmo is forever a Manhattan drink due to its association with Sex and the City. There, it represented freedom, whimsy, and sophistication. Sub out pomegranate juice for the cranberry and you have a Cospo. You can make one without citrus vodka, but trust me, it's not the same.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz citrus vodka
  • 3/4 oz orange liqueur
  • 1/2 oz fresh lime juice
  • 1 oz cranberry juice
  • Garnish: lime wheel

Preparation

  1. Fill a shaker with ice
  2. Add the vodka, orange liqueur, and juices
  3. Shake vigorously until well-chilled
  4. Strain into chilled coupe or cocktail glass
  5. Garnish with a lime wheel

Comments

  • "Why did we ever stop drinking these?” “Because everyone else started." - Miranda and Carrie, Sex and the City movie
