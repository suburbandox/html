Flip

The Flip is an ancient drink, and variations on it were drunk as far back as the 1600's, in England. In its original form, beer, rum, and sugar were heated; later versions called for a red hot poker to be plunged into the drink causing it to froth or "flip." Modern versions are generally served cold, and egg was added. For protein?
