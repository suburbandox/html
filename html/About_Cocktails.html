Kline Cocktail Compendium: A Work of Love

The Kline Cocktail Compendium represents a collaboration between father and son.
Joel is a longtime cocktail enthusiast who greatly expanded his repertoire (and the size of his cocktail cabinet) during the COVID pandemic as a respite from his work as a critical care physician. He is responsible for most of the content.
Ethan is embarking on a career in programming; this is his first attempt at developing a website using HTML/CSS. It is a work in progress. Comments appreciated!
