The Highball

The Highball is simple, but therefore requires attention to the details and qualities of the ingredients. At its most basic, it is a spirit and a mixer. The classic is whiskey and soda, but recently the Vodka/soda has gained favor especially among carb-conscious imbibers. It is most often (but not uniformly) served in a tall glass on ice. BTW, highball seems to refer to trains, highballing meaning either that the train is traveling under high pressure from the boiler, or the track ahead is open and clear.
